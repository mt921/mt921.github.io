Marci Talbot

I like developing projects which explore the cross-section between these areas, often in visual forms. This website aims to showcase some of my projects interactively.

Explore random generation through investigating different types of noise & distributions. Investigating different types of colour spaces. Incoporating elements of machine learning and neural networks with TensorFlow.JS to help develop more complex colour schemes.
